UN Sustainable Development Goal 14: Life Below Water


Oceans cover more than 70% of our planet and are home to millions of marine species. However, pollution, overfishing, and climate change are threatening marine ecosystems. Our website focuses on Sustainable Development Goal 14 (Life Below Water) and shows how students and communities can take action to protect ocean life.

Preserving Our Blue Planet

Why oceans matter


Oceans sustain life, regulate climate, and protect biodiversity.


  • Produce over 50% of the world's oxygen
  • Support billions of people
  • Regulate global climate
  • Provide food and livelihoods

Threats to Marine Life


Human activities are damaging marine ecosystems worldwide.


  • Plastic pollution
  • Overfishing
  • Climate change

Take Action


Everyone can help protect the ocean with simple daily actions.


  • Reduce plastic use
  • Join beach clean-ups
  • Support sustainable seafood
