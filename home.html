Preserving Our Blue Planet

The ocean is the lifeblood of our world, covering more than 70% of Earth’s surface and generating at least half of the oxygen we breathe. From the vibrant biodiversity of coral reefs to the vast, open currents that regulate our global climate, these waters are essential for all life on land. However, the delicate balance of marine ecosystems is currently under siege from plastic pollution, overfishing, and the rapid onset of climate change. Protecting our oceans is no longer an option it is a necessity. By making sustainable choices today, we can ensure that the "blue heart" of our planet remains resilient and thriving for generations to come.

Human activities are damaging marine ecosystems worldwide.


  • Plastic pollution
  • Overfishing

Take Action


Everyone can help protect the ocean with simple daily actions.


  • Reduce plastic use
  • Join beach clean-ups
  • Support sustainable seafood

Protect Coral Reefs


Coral reefs are vital marine ecosystems that need protection.


  • Avoid touching or stepping on reefs
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen
  • Support coral restoration efforts
